Nam Tok Recipe

  1. Ingredients

    • 1 pound Chuck Steak
    • 2 Shallots diced
    • 1/2 cup Fish Sauce
    • 1/2 cup lime juice squeezed from fresh limes
    • 2 Thai Chili Peppers minced (Two is modestly spicy, add more if you want increased spiciness).
    • 1 heaping tablespoon Toasted Rice Powder
    • 2 sprigs worth of mint leaves

  2. Instructions

    • Grill or broil the steak. I personally recommend the steaks be hickory smoked in a charcoal grill for added flavor, but grilling method ultimately doesn't matter.
    • Cut the steak into small strips.
    • Mix the steak, shallots, fish sauce, lime juice, Thai chili peppers, and toasted rice powder in a medium sized bowel.
    • Garnish each serving with equal amounts of mint. (I say garnish, but the mint leaves add essential flavor to the dish and are therefore not optional).
    • Serve with sticky rice and cucumbers.
